The caBIG® Adverse Event Reporting System (caAERS) is used to collect, process, and report adverse events that occur during clinical trials. caAERS also supports service-based integration of data from other clinical trials management systems.

This eLearning system is designed to help you acquire the skills necessary to use caAERS in your daily work. Our training modules are categorized by the role that you will be playing with caAERS.

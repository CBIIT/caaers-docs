Assign Participants to Studies Overview

The caAERS application allows a user to assign participants already loaded in the application to other studies via a web based interface.  The process occurs in two different ways, but there are 3 screens of information that need to be supplied regardless of the way you choose to assign the participant. 

The first way is to search for the participant, then search for the study, and finally review the information.  The second way is very similar, but the study is searched for before the participant is searched for. 

This online help provide information for each section but realize the process could be different depending on which way you started.

Every field in the online help that references a required field means it is required in all circumstances.  Other optional fields will also be explained.
